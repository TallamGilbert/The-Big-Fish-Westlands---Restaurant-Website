Our Story

The Big Fish Experience

Located in Westlands, Nairobi, Big Fish Restaurant is your go-to spot for fresh, affordable seafood with a Kenyan twist. Founded by siblings Bradox Osumo, Maureen Adhiambo, and their family, it grew from a small venture into a beloved dining destination.
We specialize in whole tilapia—fried, steamed, or in coconut sauce—served with ugali, chips, or kachumbari. Sourced fresh from Migori County, our generous portions and budget-friendly prices (Ksh 650-750 per meal) make us a standout in Nairobi’s food scene. Enjoy a warm, nautical-inspired ambiance or order via Bolt Food and Jumia. Open daily from 8 AM to 9 PM, we’re here to serve up great seafood, great vibes, and great memories.
